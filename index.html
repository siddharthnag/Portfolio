Logistics Team Lead @ SD Hacks 2021
May 2020 - Present

Secretary @ HKN at UCSD
May 2020 - Present

Eta Kappa Nu (IEEE-HKN) is the student honor society of IEEE and is dedicated to encouraging and recognizing excellence in IEEE fields of interest. As Secretary, I ensure the accurate dissemination of information to HKN members and other students through the weekly newsletter that broadcasts upcoming HKN technical workshops and professional and social events. In addition, maintain HKN events calendars, Facebook event pages, and mailing lists.
